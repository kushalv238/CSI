About

DJCSI’s initiative, Pixel Paranoia!

Do you have a knack for design? Are you a problem solver? Can you navigate through challenges? If your answer is yes, we have just the competition for you!

“Pixel Paranoia” is an open platform for all designers to flaunt their designing skills.

This event spans over a time period of two days, and it consists of two competitions. First is Graphic Designing and the second is UI / UX Competition, and you can participate in both.!

You will be asked to design a working prototype for your selected real-time corporate problem statement and submit your solution under the guidance of experts in the field. Some of the domains include “Food-centric E-Commerce”, “Banking”, “Education Marketplace”, and many more!

Participants can use Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD or any similar tool for UI / UX Competition. Similarly, there are no restrictions on tools used in Graphic Designing.

The participants will be felicitated with fancy merchandise and goodies, while winners will get a chance to bag exciting internships!

Guidelines

I] Graphic Designing:

01

Participants are allowed to participate solo.

02

The event is open to all willing candidates who are interested in Graphic Designing. Aspirants from diverse graduation backgrounds viz. Engineering, B.S.c., Management, etc. can apply.

03

Any of the problem statements can be picked by the participants in order of their preferences but they can opt for only one. Problem statements will be given on first come first served basis.

04

The given solution needs to be implemented and submitted in the provided time limit. Participants will be asked to present their work in specific time slots allocated after judges finish the reviewing procedure.

05

Formal communication and presentations will be conducted on Discord server.

II] UI / UX Competition:

01

Participants are allowed to participate in teams of two.

02

The event is open to all willing candidates who are interested in UI / UX.A team can be either Intra-College or Inter-College, both are permitted. Aspirants from diverse graduation backgrounds viz. Engineering, B.S.c., Management, etc. can apply.

03

Any of the problem statements can be picked by the participants in order of their preferences but they can opt for only one. Problem statements will be given on first come first served basis.

04

The given solution needs to be implemented and submitted in the provided time limit. Participants will be asked to present their work in specific time slots allocated after judges finish the reviewing procedure.

05

Formal communication and presentations will be conducted on Discord server.
